How to Check Result

Enter Your Roll / Application ID

Type your HSC Roll Number or Application ID in the search box on the NU website.

3

Submit and View

4

Complete Final Admission

If selected, fill the Final Admission Form online immediately and complete college admission before the deadline.

Frequently Asked Questions

When was the NU Honours Admission Result 2026 published?

The NU Admission Result 1st Merit List was published on 3 May 2026.

What is the SMS format to check NU result?

Type NU ATHN [your roll number] and send to 16222. Example: NU ATHN 1234567

What should I do after getting selected in the merit list?

Fill up the Final Admission Form online immediately and complete college admission before the deadline. Missing the deadline may result in loss of your seat.

Can I change my subject after the result?

Yes, you can apply for migration if seats are available. Check the Migration Result on the official NU website after it is published.

What happens if I miss the admission deadline?

You may lose your seat permanently. Always follow the dates given on the official NU website.

Where is the official NU admission link?

The official links are app.nu.edu.bd/nu-web/admissionTestResultQueryForm and www.nu.ac.bd/admissions.
